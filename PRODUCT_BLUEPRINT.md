# Product blueprint

The killer feature is not a dashboard. It is:

> Speak once on site → BuildMind creates the diary, delay, variation, hazard, timesheet note, client/HQ/architect email draft, evidence pack and searchable construction memory.

Target buyer: small-to-medium builders who hate heavy construction software but lose money through undocumented delays, missing variations and weak site records.

MVP modules:

1. Voice-to-Everything
2. Site diary
3. Delay register
4. Variation tracker
5. Safety/hazard log
6. Defects
7. Timesheets
8. Team permissions
9. Email drafts
10. Drive evidence packs
11. Construction Memory search
12. Billing/paywall
